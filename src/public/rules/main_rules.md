# Main Rules

Vanilla Tyme is a whitelisted vanilla multiplayer minecraft server. We also offer a second server hub for a creative plots server and UHC game mode.
Everyone must read and agree to the rules.

Any attempt to circumvent or find loopholes in the rules will not be tolerated. The rules are not all inclusive. If you are asked to do or not do something by staff because it's not in line with our principles, you are expected to comply. Breaking any rule(s) will result in a warning and or ban from the server.

The intention of Vanilla Tyme SMP is to foster a friendly sense of community and belonging to all members.
These rules apply to all in-game communication (text chat, voice chat, builds, signs, map art etc..) in addition to Discord text channels and voice chat.

### Joining Vanilla Tyme SMP:

1. Potential players must read all of the rules in full before applying to play.
2. All potential players must apply by joining our Discord server and filling out an application. After applying, players must be approved to play.
    - Do not ask staff to review an application.
3. Incomplete and/or low effort applications may be denied.
4. Applicants with inappropriate Minecraft usernames or skins (as deemed by staff) will not be accepted unless the name and/or skin is changed.
5. Any player found to have lied on their application prior to, or anytime after being accepted, is subject to an immediate ban.
6. Once accepted, all players must link their discord (instructions in discord \#link-account) and remain in our discord for the duration of the time they intend to play.

### Safety and Communication

1. No harassment, disrespectful, derogatory, or abusive chat towards anyone, including staff.
    - No name calling or swearing at others
    - No excessive swearing. Abusive swearing, intended to offend, intimidate or otherwise cause emotional or psychological harm is not allowed.
    - No racist, anti-LGBTQ+, sexist, anti-religious, or otherwise offensive content
    - No political or religious topics
    - No arguing in public chat/channels. You may respectfully discuss opposing views via in game dm but if someone asks you to stop, you must comply.
    - No harmful, violent, or obscene content. This includes but is not limited to pornographic material, sexual acts, violence, self harm, or drugs.
2. No Doxxing \- Doxxing is defined as sharing private or identifying information about another person. This includes their name, location, social media or any other identifying information.
3. No Spamming \- Spamming is defined as repetitive and/or irrelevant and/or unsolicited messages
4. No Advertising or Self Promotion
    - No self promotion of any type with the exception of content created on Vanilla Tyme. Vanilla Tyme related content may be shared via the community-content channel on discord.
    - No promoting or mention of other Minecraft Servers. General discussion of Hermitcraft, 2b2t,... is allowed.
    - Advertising of in game goods or services is limited to no more than once per hour in game chat. Preferably, advertisements for in game goods or services may be posted in the discord Buy Sell Trade channel.
    - No asking or begging for free items.
    - No sales of items for real currency.
5. All players must comply with the Discord EULA
6. All players must comply with the Minecraft/Microsoft EULA
7. Alternate (Alt) accounts are not allowed. Each player may use one Minecraft and one Discord account. You may change the account you use by notifying staff via support ticket. If you share the internet/live with another person who also plays on the server, please submit a ticket in our Discord to notify.
8. Players are responsible for all actions on their account, both Discord and Minecraft. Do not allow others to use your account to log into the server. If your Minecraft or Discord account becomes compromised, you should contact staff as soon as possible to notify.
9. For moderation purposes, English is the only language allowed in public game chat. Other languages are allowed via in-game private messages.

### **Support and Help**

    Our staff are available to players in game and discord.

1. Any general server related questions can be asked in the public community help channel on discord. Any staff or player can respond with relevant information in this channel.
2. **Any specific server related questions or those needing staff response should be asked via a [support ticket](https://discord.com/channels/304066050201485322/950596143258165288) in discord.** Using a support ticket will get you the quickest assistance and allows all of our staff to view and respond.
3. Do not direct message individual staff in discord. The exception to this is if you are having an issue with a staff member, in which case you may directly contact a developer.
4. If staff are in game, you may ask straightforward questions there. Staff are oftentimes busy or afk so you may not receive an answer or be asked to create a support ticket. If you do not receive an answer, create a support ticket.

### Game Play:

Our aim is to provide a server with vanilla game play. All player actions on the server are logged for moderation.

1. We enforce **fair play** \- We do not allow the use of any cheats, hacks, or exploits.
    - No use of in game bugs, glitches, or exploits. Breaking bedrock on the nether roof or at the base of the world in your claimed base area is allowed with tnt & piston.
    - No intentional lagging or crashing/trying to crash the server
    - No duplication of any items. TNT duplication is not allowed.
    - Do not attempt to bring in items from outside of the game.
    - No scripts or macros
    - No autologgers or use of any mod or script that will log you back into the game automatically.
    - No autoclickers
    - No use of any method to assist in finding any structure, item, or block such as ores or chests. Using any vanilla exploit, server exploit, client, mod, resource pack, or texture pack to see through blocks (xray) or locate desired blocks is not allowed.
    - No autominers
    - No fly hacks/ creative style flying.
    - No speed hacks
    - No PvP hacks
    - No free camera or any detaching your camera view from your character
    - The server seed is private and not to be used by players. Obtaining or attempting to obtain, having, or using the map seed through any means is not allowed. If someone shares or attempts to share the seed with you, contact staff via support ticket.
    - No griefing. Griefing is defined as causing damage or harm in any way to another player, property, or items not belonging to you.
    - No stealing. Stealing is defined as taking items that do not inherently belong to you. No one is allowed to take items from a base, chest, farm, spawn or any other area without permission from the owner. This also includes the use of farms for items or experience.
    - Do not take out or rearrange items in any container in spawn, the market, or bases that do not belong to you.
2. **Client modifications** \- The use of any client "mods" modifications or clients that give an unfair advantage to the game, including scripts, are not allowed.
   We do have a few exceptions for specific client side mods/types of mods. More info can be found [here](https://docs.vanillatymeservers.com/mods). If you are unsure if a mod is considered fair, please contact staff via support ticket.
3. **PvP** is not allowed unless all parties consent. PvP/Player versus Player is defined as harming another player in game. This includes direct harm or indirect via traps.

### Server Specifics:

1. **Spawn**
    - Spawn extends 750 blocks in all directions from x0 / z0. Building, mining, and resource gathering of any kind at spawn is only permitted in designated areas or with staff approval.
    - For events relating to holidays or other celebrations that include free items, players are only ever allowed to take 1 item (unless otherwise specified on a sign). Each player is responsible to pick up their own item. Players cannot pick up items for others.
2. **End**
    - The first dragon fight and opening of the end is scheduled after the start of each map. No one is allowed to go to the End dimension before this time.
    - Flying with Elytra is only permitted in the NORTHERN hemisphere. This will give new players without an elytra a chance to explore and collect in the other direction.
    - No personal Enderman farms. Server provides a public Enderman farm.
    - The dragon may be respawned.
    - Farms and builds of any type are not allowed on the main end island without staff approval. You may build farms in the outer end islands (x/z coords \+/- 1000). Temporary beacons are allowed to aide in mining obsidian pillars.
3. **Nether**
    - Our main nether tunnels extend north, south, east, and west from 0, 0\. Players may not build in or edit the main nether tunnels which extend to approximately \+/- 7 on each side of 0\.
    - Your nether portal must connect to the closest main tunnel and can not have perpendicular side tunnels. If the tunnel you need is already taken, you must contact the person or staff to share the tunnel.
        1. Tunnels cannot exceed the max width and height of 9x9; this includes the walls, floor and roof.
        2. Personal nether tunnels and portals must follow the proper size and layout format.
            - Personal portals must be created or moved to 115 on the y axis **in the nether** and must sync properly with your portal in the Overworld.
            - Personal nether tunnels are spaced every 10 blocks. Your personal tunnel should end in coordinates 1 and 9\. Example: tunnel goes from 721 to 729
            - The blocks on the 0 cords should be left as is. Example: Block 720 and 730 are left netherrack
    - Personal portal hubs must be on the nether roof and are restricted to your personal base (750x750 in the overworld).
4. **Bases** \- player claimed areas in the overworld

    - Bases shall not exceed 750 blocks squared (750x750) in size.
    - Ensure your base is marked with pillars/signs in each corner or a full border.
    - In addition to in-world markings, bases can also be marked with a Bluemap marker by placing a renamed banner in the place you want it to show. Names must be appropriate. The marker will automatically add your ign. One player can place up to 5 map markers.
    - Do not break or attempt to break another players banner marker
    - Prior to claiming your base, thoroughly scout the area, including caves, to ensure the area isn't already claimed. Use common sense. If there are torches, signs, crops planted, livestock in pens, etc., it's obvious the area is inhabited. If you are in doubt, contact a staff member via support ticket for assistance.
    - Players are allowed to claim structures outside of their main base. You may claim one of any individual type of structure. Examples include an ocean monument, nether fortress, or pillager tower. After claiming clearly with signs, players are expected to use structures within 30 days.

### Market & Economy:

1. A designated market area is provided at spawn for all players to participate in after approval.
2. Players cannot set up shops, signs, chests, or advertisements in any other part of spawn or public server area without permission or in a predesignated area. This includes the Spawn portion of the nether. This includes the overworld, nether, and main end island. THe only exception is within your own market plot/stall.
3. Players are required to read the Market Rules before starting their own Market Stall or Shop in spawn. Click on the **"Market Rules" tab** for complete rules and information.
4. Players are allowed to sell items from their base or personal nether tunnel area.

### Lag

We try to minimize lag as much as possible to ensure the most optimal experience for all players

1. No chunk loaders of any kind.
2. No bypassing the vanilla entity cramming in any way. We do not allow stacking minecarts.
3. Auto collection utilizing minecarts or hoppers shall be limited to as few as possible.
4. Hoppers should be covered with solid blocks when applicable to reduce lag.
5. No mega item sorters or excessively large redstone builds. If a farm or build is deemed to be causing significant lag, staff may ask you to fix or remove it.
6. Mob farms must have an on/off switch where possible. AFKing at hostile mob farms is allowed, only when mobs are dying immediately and not building up. Afk'ing, farms must have an on/off switch and a kill switch. Autoclickers are not allowed AFK.
7. All bases, whether they are single player or shared between multiple players, may only have a maximum of 100 animals and 100 villagers. Staff reserve the right to eliminate mobs over the limit without prior notice.
8. Villagers
    - Villager breeders must not allow over 24 villagers to build up at a time.
    - Villagers used for trading must be optimized. [Read all about it](https://docs.vanillatymeservers.com/villagers)
9. Guardian farms cannot exceed 20x20x20 in size (this refers to the Guardian spawning area).
10. Staff reserve the right to ensure lag is minimized. Staff may disable any farm that is causing significant lag or otherwise breaking the rules.

### Inactivity:

1. After 30 days of not logging in, a player with the Member rank is considered inactive. Any base owned by an inactive member rank player is subject for removal, unless staff is notified or a post has been made in the inactivity support ticket. Supporter's bases are safe from inactivity rollback.
2. At no point can another player claim an inactive base or take items from it.
3. Extra claims of structures from any rank player outside of their main base are not safe from inactivity. Examples: nether fortress, ocean monument. If you've claimed a structure and not used it in more than 60 days, staff may reclaim it.

### Proof:

### Punishments:

1. Warnings: Depending on severity of the infraction, players may be given a verbal or written warning. After 3 warnings, a player will be banned.
2. Bans: Depending on severity of the infraction, a player may be immediately banned without warning. Bans are permanent but may be appealed once. If an appeal is denied, the ban is permanent without further opportunity for appeal.
